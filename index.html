The Nigerian Jollof Rice

Rice is one of the most common staple foods in Nigeria. Most families eat a rice dish at least twice a week, either for lunch or for dinner.
We have different ways of cooking rice in Nigeria the major ones are

  • White rice served with stew or sauce
  • Jollof rice
  • Fried rice

However theNigerian Jollof Riceis unarguable the best when compared with other west-african countries. The Nigerian Jollof Rice is a rich
and incredibly tasty west African one-pot Meal. It is a very versatile dish, and it is usually made from scratch using:

Jollof Rice Ingredients

  1. Rice
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Pimento peppers
  4. Tomato paste
  5. Scotch bonnet
  6. Onions
  7. Salt
  8. and other spices
Although the Nigerian Jollof appears to be a dish eaten by all tribes in the country, however each tribe also have dishes that are speculiar to their tribe.
